Official French Certification

DELF — Diplôme d'Études en Langue Française

DELF is the internationally recognised French language diploma issued by France Éducation International on behalf of the French Ministry of National Education. Valid in over 170 countries, it is accepted by universities, governments, immigration bodies, and employers worldwide.

Unlike a scored test, DELF is a pass/fail diploma with lifetime validity — it never expires. It tests all four language competencies: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

  • DELF Junior — For school-age learners (A1 to B2), with age-appropriate themes and tasks
  • DELF Tout Public — For adults (A1 to B2), covering general and professional French
  • Levels: A1 (beginner) through B2 (upper intermediate)
  • Recognised for university admission, study-abroad applications, and immigration
  • At Apprendre, our Alliance Française certified teachers cover all four exam skills with structured mock exams and targeted feedback sessions

Immigration & University Assessment

TEF / TCF — French Proficiency Tests

TEF (Test d'Évaluation de Français) and TCF (Test de Connaissance du Français) are score-based French proficiency assessments. Unlike DELF, they award a score that maps to a CEFR level rather than a permanent diploma.

These tests are essential for specific goals: Canadian or Quebec immigration, French citizenship applications, and international students applying to French universities.

  • TEF Canada — Accepted for Express Entry, Canadian permanent residency, and citizenship
  • TCF Canada / TCF Québec — Required for IRCC and Quebec immigration (RAMQ) processes
  • TCF pour la DAP — Required for international students applying to French higher-education institutions
  • Scores are valid for 2 years from the exam date
  • At Apprendre, we offer focused coaching for all TEF and TCF formats — contact us to discuss your specific goal

Curriculum-Aligned Support

School Tutoring

Our school tutoring programme is designed to complement the French curriculum followed by ICSE, CBSE, and IGCSE students. Sessions align closely with what students are studying in class, building both exam readiness and genuine language confidence at the same time.

We offer small-batch and individual sessions to maximise personal attention. Our teachers are experienced in school examination formats and provide regular practice with past papers, error analysis, and detailed feedback.

  • Covers grammar, reading comprehension, guided composition, and oral preparation
  • Syllabus-aligned for ICSE, CBSE, and IGCSE French
  • Timed mock papers with detailed error analysis
  • Builds examination strategy alongside language skills
  • Small batches (max 4 students) for focused, personalised attention

French for the Love of It

Casual French

Not everyone learns French for an exam — and that's perfectly fine. Our Casual French courses are designed for learners who want to enjoy the language without the pressure of a formal assessment. Whether you're a complete beginner, a curious parent, or simply a Francophile, there's a place for you here.

From our lively Summer Camps for children — packed with games, songs, and cultural activities — to our year-round Beginner Regular batches, Casual French is all about fun, communication, and a genuine love for the language.

  • No prior French knowledge required for beginner batches
  • Summer Camp — Immersive, activity-based learning during school holidays for children
  • Beginner Regular — New batches starting throughout the year; admissions always open
  • Real-world communication focus: greetings, travel, everyday conversation
  • Interactive methods — role-plays, discussions, videos, songs, and cultural activities
